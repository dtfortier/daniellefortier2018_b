Recent Projects

I am a user experience/user interface designer based in Buffalo, NY, currently working for Localedge. I firmly believe that design is a process and a story in progress - never an end result. For this reason I strive to familiarize myself with the development world as much as the product manager’s needs and the user’s experience to ensure I have a well rounded understanding of the application so that I can help deliver a product where all parties can benefit.
I am passionate about clean, simple design, and I really like creating elements that help people make their lives a little easier.
In my free time I enjoy painting, photography, running, hiking, skiing, traveling and fiddling with my guitar & piano.
